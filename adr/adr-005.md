## Context

ADOS Profile v1.23 stores Project-specific obligations in `APP-REQS.md`. The auditor supports App, Research, Runtime, Profile, Prompt, and Other Archetypes.

## Decision

Use `Project Requirement` as the general concept and `PROJECT_REQUIREMENTS_MODEL.md` as the explanatory document.

`App Requirement` remains a possible subtype only when the obligation is inherently App-specific.

## Alternatives

- Retain APP terminology for every Project.
- Remove requirements concepts.

## Consequences

- Source requirements must be reviewed rather than copied.
- Archetype-specific requirement subtypes remain possible.
- Banks and reports use Project-level terminology.
