## Context

Project quality can improve on one dimension while degrading on another. A flat checklist or single score cannot explain mixed evolution.

## Decision

Use Semantic Meaning Space, Goal Figure, Goal Surface, Goal Attractor, Attractor Basin, Coordinate Dimensions, Coordinates, and Metrics to interpret movement across stages and versions.

The geometry is operational and semantic. It is not presented as literal physical dynamics.

## Alternatives

- Use one total quality score only.
- Use unstructured prose without stable coordinates.

## Consequences

- Reports show coordinate-level direction and rationale.
- Metric Bank coordinates derive from Goal and Objectives.
- Confidence and evidence status remain separate from scores.
