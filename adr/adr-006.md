## Context

The auditor needs enough classification to focus evaluation without recreating an oversized profile catalog.

## Decision

Archetypes:

- App
- Research
- Runtime
- Profile
- Prompt
- Other

Domains:

- Business
- Business-Trading
- Science
- Education
- Software-Engineering
- Governance
- Other

One primary Archetype and one primary Domain are selected for an evaluation.

## Alternatives

- Use ten or more specialized Archetypes.
- Combine Archetype and Domain into one label.
- Treat Package as an Archetype.

## Consequences

- Package remains a materialization form, not a Project type.
- Profiles remain understandable and extensible.
- Other requires explicit rationale.
