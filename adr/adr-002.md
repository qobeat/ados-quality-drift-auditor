## Context

ADOS concepts include PDD, Requirements, Atomic Work, Directives, Evidence Ledger, and Execution Trace. Earlier auditor architecture risked turning these concepts into mandatory workflow machinery.

## Decision

The auditor may inspect and evaluate execution concepts when present, but it does not require or orchestrate:

- PDDs;
- Work Plans;
- Atomic Work;
- Apply/Verify pairs;
- claim or evidence ledgers;
- package validators;
- release workflows.

## Alternatives

- Enforce one ADOS workflow for every evaluated Project.
- Remove execution concepts from the glossary.

## Consequences

- The glossary can remain ADOS-specific without over-governing the agent.
- Archetype profiles and report policy determine applicability.
- Missing execution surfaces are not defects unless a governing source requires them.
