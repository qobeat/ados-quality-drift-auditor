## Context

v0.1 over-centered evaluator bookkeeping. v0.2 needs structured output without prescribing internal agent state management.

## Decision

The required machine output is one schema-conformant evaluation report containing:

- sources and versions;
- semantic frame;
- glossary terms used;
- Archetype and Domain selection;
- coordinate evaluations;
- drift comparisons;
- Problem Pattern matches;
- Baseline recommendation;
- corrective prompt;
- uncertainties and final assessment.

Claim or Evidence Ledgers may be evaluated as source artifacts but are not mandatory auditor outputs.

## Alternatives

- Require separate claim and evidence ledgers.
- Produce only unstructured Markdown.

## Consequences

- Package remains lightweight.
- Reports still expose support and uncertainty.
- Host agents may use internal notes at their discretion.
